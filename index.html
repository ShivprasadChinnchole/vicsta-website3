About VICSTA

Empowering the next generation of tech innovators

Who We Are

VICSTA (Vishwakarma IOT Cyber Security Tech Association) is a premier technology club at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology. We are dedicated to fostering innovation, learning, and collaboration in the fields of Internet of Things (IoT) and Cybersecurity.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant ecosystem where students can explore cutting-edge technologies, develop practical skills, and contribute to solving real-world problems through innovative IoT and cybersecurity solutions.
